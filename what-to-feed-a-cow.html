What to Feed a Cow

Cows need a balanced diet to stay healthy and produce milk or meat efficiently.

  • Fresh grass and hay (their primary food)
  • Silage (fermented, high-moisture stored fodder)
  • Grain (corn, oats, barley)
  • Mineral supplements
  • Clean water (always available)
